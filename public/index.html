The decision engine

Measured judgment, not a guessing receptionist

The hard part of a medical call isn't the conversation — it's deciding what happens next. AthenaBot routes against a versioned decision tree your clinicians review and sign off on. Try it.

911

Seek emergency care now

Red-flag symptoms — chest pain, stroke signs, severe bleeding, safety risk — trigger an immediate hand-off and priority staff alert.

Urgent

Visit urgent care

Acute but not life-threatening. Caller is directed to urgent care with the nearest location and a logged follow-up.

Clinician

Speak with a clinician

Medication questions, worsening symptoms, post-op concerns — warm-transferred to the nurse line with the intake summary.

Schedule

Book an appointment

Routine and follow-up requests are booked directly into the calendar, no human needed.
